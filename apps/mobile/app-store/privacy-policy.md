# K12Buddy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

K12Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children under 13. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational mobile application.

## COPPA Compliance

K12Buddy complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We take special care to protect children's privacy:

- **Parental Consent**: For users under 13, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information.
- **Limited Data Collection**: We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our educational services.
- **No Behavioral Advertising**: We do not serve behavioral or targeted advertising to children.
- **Parental Access**: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time.

## Information We Collect

### Information Provided by Users

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, grade level
- **Parent/Guardian Information**: For users under 13, parent email for consent verification
- **Educational Data**: Subject preferences, learning progress

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (anonymized)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information when errors occur (anonymized)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Precise geolocation data
- Contact lists or address books
- Photos or videos (except for homework scanning, which is processed and not stored)
- Third-party social media data

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Educational Services**: Personalize learning experiences and track progress
2. **Improve Our App**: Understand how users interact with features
3. **Ensure Safety**: Moderate content and protect users
4. **Communicate**: Send important updates (with parental consent for children)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers**: Companies that help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict privacy agreements
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect safety
- **With Consent**: Only when parents/guardians explicitly agree

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication systems
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

## Parental Rights

Parents/guardians of children under 13 can:

- **Access**: Review their child's personal information
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request deletion of their child's account and data
- **Opt-out**: Revoke consent for data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@k12buddy.com

## Your Rights

All users can:

- Access their data
- Export their data
- Delete their account
- Opt-out of non-essential communications

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication (SOC 2 Type II certified)
- **OpenAI**: AI tutoring (content processed, not stored)
- **Sentry**: Crash reporting (anonymized)
