# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 19, 2026**

K12Buddy AI Learning Tutor ("K12Buddy", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children under 13. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and password when creating an account
- **Profile Information**: Display name, grade level, preferred subjects, and learning style
- **Birth Date**: To verify age and ensure COPPA compliance
- **Parent Contact**: Email for parent/guardian consent (users under 13)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features used, session duration, and learning progress
- **Chat History**: Conversations with the AI tutor (retained for 90 days)

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Precise GPS location (only approximate city-level with consent)
- Social Security numbers or financial information
- Contacts or phone call logs
- Microphone recordings (unless explicit voice feature is enabled)

---

## 2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

K12Buddy is designed for K-12 students. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- **Parental Consent**: Users under 13 require verified parental consent before full account access
- **Data Minimization**: We only collect information necessary for educational purposes
- **No Behavioral Advertising**: We never use children's data for targeted advertising
- **Parental Controls**: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information
- **Right to Refuse**: Parents may refuse further collection of their child's data

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide personalized AI tutoring experiences
- Track learning progress and achievements
- Improve our educational content and features
- Communicate important updates about the service
- Ensure platform safety and prevent misuse

We do **NOT** use your information to:
- Sell to third parties
- Display behavioral advertising
- Create marketing profiles

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share information with:
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting, authentication, and analytics (bound by data protection agreements)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect user safety
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of merger or acquisition (with notice)

We **never** sell personal information to third parties.

---

## 5. Data Security

We protect your information using:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
- Secure authentication with hashed passwords
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls limiting employee data access
- Row-level security in our database

---

## 6. Data Retention

- **Chat History**: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
- **Account Data**: Retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request account and data deletion
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics data collection
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, visit Settings > Privacy or contact us at privacy@k12buddy.com.

---

## 8. Location Data

If you enable location sharing:
- We only collect **approximate location** (city-level)
- Used to personalize educational content (local events, regional curriculum)
- You can revoke consent at any time in Settings > Privacy
- We never track precise GPS coordinates

---

## 9. Third-Party Services

K12Buddy uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Anthropic Claude**: AI tutoring (no personal data shared beyond conversation context)
- **Expo**: App distribution and updates

Each service maintains its own privacy policy and is GDPR/CCPA compliant.
